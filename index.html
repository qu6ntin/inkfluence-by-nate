The Artist

Where Your Tattoo Journey Begins

Inkfluence by Nate is a custom tattoo studio rooted in the Chippewa of the Thames First Nation community in Ontario. Every piece starts as a conversation — your idea, your story — and becomes a one-of-a-kind design made to last.

From fine-line and black & grey to bold colour work and cover-ups, the focus is always on clean, thoughtful artistry in a relaxed, welcoming space.

  • Custom, original designs
  • Sterile, single-use equipment
  • Clear aftercare guidance
  • Friendly, judgment-free studio
